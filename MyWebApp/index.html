About

This map was designed with the goal to summarize the Museum of Southwestern Biology's efforts to capture mammals and their associated parts in the country of Mongolia. This map can currently be filtered by year and by species. It is available to any user but is intended to inform and connect the citizens of Mongolia to the research that is done on their land. This map aims to make available information that is often difficult to access in a interactive and educational format.
